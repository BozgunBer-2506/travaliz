Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

TravelMirror is a travel search demo application that aggregates flight, hotel, and car rental data via the Amadeus Self-Service API. It is built and maintained by thebozgun.com. Questions can be directed to contact@thebozgun.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the service:

  • Search queries: destination, dates, and passenger counts entered during a search.
  • Booking data: name, email, phone, and last 4 digits of card number submitted during a demo booking. Full card details are never stored.
  • Usage data: pages visited and session information stored in a secure HTTP-only cookie.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display relevant flight, hotel, and car rental results.
  • To generate and display a demo booking reference number.
  • To improve the application and fix bugs.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Amadeus API: travel data is fetched from the Amadeus Self-Service API. Their privacy policy applies to data transmitted during API calls.
  • Vercel: the application is hosted on Vercel. Server logs and request metadata may be retained per Vercel's data processing terms.

5. Demo Nature of This Service

TravelMirror is a demonstration project. Bookings made through this platform are not real reservations. No payments are processed. Booking data is stored temporarily in an ephemeral SQLite database and is not retained between server restarts.

6. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to maintain application state. This cookie contains no personally identifiable information. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Data Retention

Search queries and booking data are stored only for the duration of the server session. No long-term data retention is in place for this demo application.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@thebozgun.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions: contact@thebozgun.com
